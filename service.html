Introduction

Home Hunter is a real estate platform designed to help users find their perfect home, whether for rent or purchase. The platform connects buyers, renters, and property owners, providing a seamless property search experience with advanced filters, virtual tours, and market insights.

Services Provided

1. Property Listings

* Displays available homes, apartments, and commercial properties for sale or rent.
* Includes high-quality images, descriptions, and pricing details.

2. Advanced Search & Filters

* Allows users to filter properties based on location, budget, size, type, and amenities.
* Provides an interactive map view for better location-based searches.

3. Virtual Tours & Images

* Offers 360Â° virtual tours and video walkthroughs for an immersive property experience.
* Provides multiple images from different angles for better visualization.

4. Contact Property Owners & Agents

* Enables direct messaging or call options to connect with property owners and real estate agents.
* Includes verified contact details for transparency.

5. Price Comparison & Market Insights

* Compares similar properties in the same area to help users make informed decisions.
* Provides real-time market trends, price fluctuations, and investment opportunities.

6. Loan & Mortgage Calculator

* Helps users estimate loan eligibility, monthly mortgage payments, and total interest costs.
* Provides guidance on financial planning for property purchases.

7. User Reviews & Ratings

* Allows buyers and renters to rate properties and agents based on their experiences.
* Helps new users make better decisions with genuine reviews.

8. Saved Listings & Alerts

* Users can save favorite properties for future reference.
* Sends notifications for new listings, price drops, and changes in saved properties.

9. Neighborhood Insights

* Provides details on nearby schools, hospitals, transportation, crime rates, and lifestyle amenities.
* Helps users choose a property based on the surrounding environment.

10. Secure Transactions & Legal Support

* Ensures secure and verified property transactions.
* Provides basic legal guidance for rental agreements, purchases, and registrations.
